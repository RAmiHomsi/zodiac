Cancer

Cancer is a water sign and is associated with the element of water. People born under this sign are often sensitive, nurturing, and intuitive. They are very in tune with their emotions and the emotions of those around them. They value their home and family life, and often prioritize their relationships above all else. They are often very caring and compassionate, and are known for their ability to comfort and support others. However, they can also be moody and insecure at times, and can struggle with setting healthy boundaries in their relationships. They value security and stability, and are often very sentimental and nostalgic.
