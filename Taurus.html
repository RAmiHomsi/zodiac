Taurus

Taurus is an earth sign and is associated with the element of earth. People born under this sign are often practical, patient, and reliable. They enjoy the pleasures of life and appreciate the beauty around them. They are also known for their determination and persistence, and once they set their mind to something, they will work tirelessly to achieve it. However, they can also be stubborn and resistant to change, which can sometimes hold them back from reaching their full potential. They are often very sensual and enjoy indulging in the finer things in life, such as good food, wine, and art. They value stability and security and are often very loyal and committed to their relationships.
